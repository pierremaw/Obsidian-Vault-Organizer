Transactions get inserted via a 
Priority Gas Auction (PGA).

The mempool is a priority queue.

Transactions that have high gas,
get placed at the front.

Transactions that have low gas, 
get placed at the rear. ^Dw6VEIuY

The aim is to calculate the gas fee needed to ensure your transaction gets included before any competing arbs. 

So you've have to see if there are any similar arb transactions.
